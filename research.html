Development, implementation, and findings from our student career-planning tool.

The Story of Our Tool

The College-to-Career Tool was developed to help sociology students better understand their career options, identify professional development opportunities, and connect their academic experiences to future goals. Through surveys and student feedback, our team is working to create resources that make career planning more accessible and effective.

What the Tool Offers

The College-to-Career Tool provides sociology students with resources and guidance throughout their career development journey. Students can explore career pathways, identify personal strengths, develop transferable skills, build resumes and cover letters, prepare for career fairs, learn job search strategies, and market their sociology degree to employers.

The tool also provides information on professional development opportunities, community outreach, undergraduate research experiences, graduate school preparation, and personalized career game plans that help students connect their academic experiences to future goals.
